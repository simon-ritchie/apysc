<hr>

**[Examples]**

```py
>>> import apysc as ap
>>> _ = ap.Stage()
>>> datetime_1: ap.DateTime = ap.DateTime(2022, 12, 7)
>>> datetime_2: ap.DateTime = ap.DateTime(2022, 12, 6)
```
